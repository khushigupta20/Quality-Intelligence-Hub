Quality Intelligence Hub

Your unified AI-powered quality engineering workspace — defect analysis and requirements review in one place.

🔬 Defect Root Cause Analysis

Upload your ALM defect files and get instant automated analysis.

  • Upload CSV or Excel defect exports
  • Severity, status & module distribution charts
  • Automatic defect grouping by keyword patterns
  • RCA candidates with 5-Whys analysis
  • Preventive measures with immediate & long-term actions
  • Download comprehensive PDF report

📋 Requirements Analyzer

Use the IBM ICA Agent to analyze, translate, and validate your requirements.

  • Translate — Convert requirements to English from any language
  • Analyze — Check clarity, completeness & ambiguities
  • Edge Cases — Identify boundary conditions & negative scenarios
  • Review Tests — Review test cases against requirements
  • Validate — Check consistency between requirements & test cases

🚀 Quick Start

  1. Configure ICA Agent — Go to Settings in the sidebar and enter your IBM ICA endpoint URL and API key to enable Requirements Analysis.
  2. Defect RCA — Click "Upload & Analyze" in the sidebar, drag and drop your defect CSV or Excel file, and click "Start Analysis".
  3. Requirements — Select any Requirements task from the sidebar, paste or upload your requirements text, and click Run.
  4. Download — After Defect RCA, download your PDF report from the Results tab.
